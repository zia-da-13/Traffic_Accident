In this decomposition tree, you can view the count of different types of accidents. Click on the "Total Accidents" bar and then choose 'Y' (yes) or 'N' (no) to view accidents with/without property damage. Next, choose 'Y' or 'N' to view accidents involving/not involving hit and runs. Then, you'll see a list of cities. Click the city you'd like to view and you'll see the total of each collision type appear for the selected city
